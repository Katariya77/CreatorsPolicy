Last Updated: November 2025

1. General Overview

The ZNT Creator Program is an exclusive partnership initiative designed to support, promote, and collaborate with content creators who align with our esports values and community standards.

By joining, creators agree to uphold the professionalism, integrity, and reputation of ZNT Esports in all their public activities.

3. Representation & Branding

  • Creators may represent themselves as "ZNT Creator" or "Partnered with ZNT Esports."
  • Use of official logos, overlays, or branding assets must follow ZNT's Brand Kit (provided upon acceptance).
  • All ZNT-related content must reflect the team positively and avoid controversies.

Any unauthorized use of the ZNT name or assets (merch, logos, sponsors) for profit or misrepresentation is strictly prohibited.

4. Monetization & Sponsorships

  • Creators are free to have their own sponsors or brand deals, provided they do not conflict with ZNT's existing sponsors or partnerships.
  • All collaboration or sponsorship requests involving ZNT's name or logo must be approved by management in writing.
  • ZNT may offer exclusive sponsorship opportunities or revenue-sharing deals â€” participation is optional but subject to internal terms.

5. Content & Media Rights

  • All content created under ZNT collaborations remains property of the creator, but ZNT retains the right to repost, feature, or showcase it for promotional purposes (with proper credit).
  • ZNT may request certain branding integration (intro/outro, hashtag, watermark) for uniformity.
  • Content that violates copyright laws, community guidelines, or ZNT standards may be requested to be removed or edited.

6. Activity & Participation

  • Creators must remain reasonably active on their primary platform.
  • Extended inactivity (30+ days) without notice may result in temporary removal from the program.
  • Participation in team events, tournaments, or campaigns is encouraged but voluntary unless otherwise agreed upon.

7. Confidentiality

Any non-public information shared by ZNT (plans, line-ups, internal projects, sponsorship details, etc.) is strictly confidential.

Sharing internal discussions or leaks without permission will lead to immediate removal from the program.

8. Termination & Removal

ZNT reserves the right to:

  • Terminate a creator's partnership at any time, with or without notice, for violations of policy, inactivity, or misconduct.
  • Remove any member using ZNT's name for personal drama, false claims, or unauthorized business.
  • Reconsider partnership after a minimum of 30 days if the creator wishes to re-apply.

Creators may also leave the program voluntarily at any time by notifying management.

9. Communication & Support

  • All official communication must take place through ZNT's Discord server or verified staff contacts.
  • Creators are expected to maintain professional communication with ZNT staff and other creators.
  • Support will be available for branding, collaborations, and guidance through the Creator Support Team.

10. Agreement

By joining the ZNT Creator Program, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agreed to follow all terms listed above.

ZNT Esports reserves the right to update or modify these policies at any time to maintain fairness and integrity across the program.
